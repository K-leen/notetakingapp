How We Began

A Dream of Dignity for Every Child

In 2012, Mama Joyce Ndaa opened the doors of Joyous Children's Home in a modest house in Malindi with just seven children and an unshakeable belief: every child deserves to be loved.

What began as one woman's response to the children sleeping on matteress laid down has grown into a thriving home for over 90+ children — a real family, complete with laughter, homework, prayers, and shared meals.

Today, Joyous Children's Home stands out, known not just for shelter, but for transforming lives from the inside out — nurturing confidence, curiosity, and joy in every child.

Our Journey

2012

The Home Opens

Mama Joyce Ndaa opens the first home in M'Chembe with 7 children, no volunteers, and a lot of faith.

2012

Registered with the Government

Joyous Children's Home officially registered as a charitable children's institution under Kenya law.

2015

New Building & Expansion

With donor support, we built a new wing to accommodate 60+ children and a dedicated learning centre.

2018

Vocational Training Launched

Skills training programme for teens aged 15–18 launched in partnership with local colleges.

2020

90+ Children, Growing Strong

Joyous now shelters over 90+ children and maintains a vibrant alumni network of no graduates yet.
