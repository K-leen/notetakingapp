Frequently Asked Questions

Can I visit the children? +
Yes! Visits are welcome but must be arranged in advance. Please contact us to schedule, and we will ensure a warm, structured, and meaningful visit for everyone involved.
How do I know my donation reaches the children? +
We publish annual reports and send regular updates to our donors. 100% of donations go directly to the children's needs. We also welcome unannounced visits from donors who wish to see impact firsthand.
Can I sponsor a specific child? +
Yes! Child sponsorship is one of our most impactful programmes. You receive updates, school reports, and artwork from your sponsored child. Contact us to learn more.
Do you accept international volunteers? +
Absolutely. We welcome international volunteers who commit to at least 4 weeks. We can assist with orientation and help with local accommodation options. Fill in our volunteer form to get started.
Are you registered and regulated? +
Yes. Joyous Children's Home is fully registered with the Kenya government as a Charitable Children's Institution and is regulated by the Department of Children Services. We hold all required permits and undergo regular inspections.
