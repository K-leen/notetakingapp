Get Involved

There are so many ways to show up for a child who needs you. Whether you have time, skills, resources, or simply love to share — there is a place for you at Joyous.

How You Can Help

Donate

A financial gift — however small — provides meals, schooling, and care for our children every single day.

  • One-time or monthly giving
  • M-Pesa, card, bank transfer
  • Sponsor a specific child
