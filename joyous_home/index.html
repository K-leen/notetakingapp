Our Mission

Rooted in Love, Growing with Purpose

At Joyous Children's Home, we believe that every child — regardless of background — deserves safety, dignity, education, and love. We walk alongside Kenya's most vulnerable children, not just caring for their needs today, but building their foundations for tomorrow.

How We Care

🏠

Safe Shelter

A warm, secure home where children feel truly safe, valued, and part of a loving family.

📚

Quality Education

All children are enrolled in school and supported with tutoring, mentorship, and learning materials.

💛

Emotional Wellbeing

Professional counselling and community support help every child heal, grow, and thrive.

🌱

Life Skills

We equip older children with vocational training and skills to stand strong in the world.

🎨

Arts & Play

Creative expression, music, and play are at the heart of how children at Joyous grow and heal.

🤝

Community Ties

We work hand-in-hand with local families and organisations to keep children connected to their roots.

"These children didn't lose their future — we just needed to find it together."
